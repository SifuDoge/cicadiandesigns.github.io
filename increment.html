INCREMENT

The Simple Counter
Increment is a streamlined digital counter designed for high-performance tracking. Built with a minimalist aesthetic, it removes the friction of complex menus to focus on a single objective: the count.
  • Tactile Interaction: Optimized for rapid, single-handed input.
  • Absolute Privacy: All logic and data remain local to your device.
  • Persistence: Progress is preserved across sessions automatically.
